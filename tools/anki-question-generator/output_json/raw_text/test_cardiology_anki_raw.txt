CARDIOLOGY NOTES

HEART FAILURE:
Systolic HF: reduced EF (< 40%). Symptoms: dyspnea, orthopnea, PND, fatigue.
BNP elevated. CXR: cardiomegaly, pulmonary edema, Kerley B lines.
Treatment: ACE inhibitor, beta-blocker, diuretics, spironolactone.
EF < 35%: ICD for sudden cardiac death prevention.

HYPERTENSION:
Stage 1: 130-139/80-89. Stage 2: >=140/>=90.
First-line: thiazides, ACE-I, ARBs, CCBs.
Hypertensive urgency: no end-organ damage. Emergency: end-organ damage (papilledema, AKI, MI).
IV labetalol or nitroprusside for emergency.
