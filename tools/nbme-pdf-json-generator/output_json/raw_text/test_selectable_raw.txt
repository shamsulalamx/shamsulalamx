## Page 1

1. A 28-year-old woman presents with 3 weeks of low mood, fatigue,
insomnia, and feelings of worthlessness. Which of the following is
the most likely diagnosis?
A. Adjustment disorder
B. Major depressive disorder
C. Persistent depressive disorder
D. Bipolar I disorder
E. Grief reaction
Correct Answer: B
Educational Objective: Diagnose major depressive disorder.
Correct Explanation: Patient meets DSM-5 criteria with 5+ SIGECAPS for 2 weeks.
Incorrect Explanations:
A. Adjustment disorder requires an identifiable stressor within 3 months.
C. PDD requires 2+ years duration.
D. No manic episodes reported.
E. Grief is not a disorder.
Review Pearl: MDD requires 5 of 9 SIGECAPS for at least 2 weeks.
